About Outsider Guide

What is Outsider Guide? We hear you cry!

Outsider guide is a passion project. Outsider guide is a ridiculous adventure. Outsider guide is that little piece in all of us that says balls to working in a office I want to feel small!

Hand built by one man, this website will be a haven for those folk who never felt quite at home in a 2 bed flat in the city. We aim to get out there and inspire you to do the same. After-all, the world isn't in your mobile phone or on your laptop its out there and its free! Get that! In this day and age, something that actually costs nothing.

Come with us as we meet folks, try cool stuff and learn a helluva lot on the way. The more the merrier so get in touch and get outside!
